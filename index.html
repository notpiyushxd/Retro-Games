Pac-Man

Description

Pac-Man is a maze arcade game developed and released by Namco in 1980. The player controls Pac-Man, who must eat all the dots inside an enclosed maze while avoiding four colored ghosts.

How to Play

Space Invaders

Description

Space Invaders is a 1978 shoot 'em up arcade game developed by Tomohiro Nishikado. It was manufactured and sold by Taito in Japan.

High Scores

  • PlayerX - 12500
  • PlayerY - 9500

How to Play

Move your laser cannon left and right. Shoot the descending aliens. Avoid their shots and don't let them reach the bottom!

Donkey Kong

Description

Donkey Kong is an arcade game released by Nintendo in 1981. It is an early example of the platform game genre, as the gameplay focuses on maneuvering the main character across a series of platforms while dodging and jumping over obstacles.

High Scores

  • JumpMan - 15000
  • PaulineFan - 11000

How to Play

Move Jumpman with the joystick and use the button to jump. Climb ladders, jump over barrels, and rescue Pauline!
